Your AI Receptionist FAQ

What is the AI Receptionist?

An intelligent call answering service that handles incoming calls 24/7. It answers instantly and engages in natural-sounding conversations to understand caller intent, keeping your business accessible and professional around the clock.

How can it make my daily business easier?

It manages routine inquiries so your team can focus on core tasks. By providing instant responses, capturing detailed call summaries (sent directly to your email), and answering multiple calls simultaneously, it keeps your business organized without adding extra work.

Can it handle more than one caller at once?

Yes. The AI Virtual Receptionist processes multiple inquiries simultaneously. Even during peak times, every caller is answered promptly and professionally.

How does it handle questions it cannot answer?

The system supports custom forwarding rules. If an inquiry requires personal attention, the call is routed to the appropriate human contact. Otherwise, it records a detailed call summary and emails it to you.

Is technical knowledge required to set it up?

Not at all. The AI is designed for intuitive use setting up in just a few minutes safely without any IT skills. You can simply connect your website to automatically import business details.

Can I keep my existing phone number?

Yes. You can easily keep your current business number by setting up call forwarding to your new AI phone number.
